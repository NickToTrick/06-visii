Visii collections

Curation on-the-fly with automated visually relevant suggestions.

Extend existing collections, looks and visual merchandising based on products your customers like.

Try Collections

Visii Collections enhances your curated groups of products by extending them with automatically generated suggestions that match their style.

On average our customers save 80 hours per month of manual merchandising tasks and can double their SKU's without additional staff.

Typical applications include: automated visual merchandising, 'Shop The Look' functionality and extending customers' favourites or wishlists with many more targeted and personalised suggestions.

  • faster
  • more scalable

How Collections Works

  • Stand-alone WebApp

    Showcase the best sellers and use Collections to create more.

    Ensure all your products become best sellers by giving them a chance. Collections will find entire outfits that match the products you want to stand out.

    Our WebApp is the perfect stand-alone landing page from mailing or social media campaigns as it offers immediate personalisation for your customers.

  • Automatically Curated Lists

    If a customer still hasn't found the right item, let Collections guide them.

    Leverage your images to find related products rather than hand selecting them yourself. Product pages will always present highly relevant and up-to-date suggestions for your customers.
